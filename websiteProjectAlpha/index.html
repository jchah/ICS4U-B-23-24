Club Activities

Tournaments

We run five-week CFC/FIDE-rated competitive tournaments on Monday evenings starting at 7:30 p.m.

We typically have three sections of players - Crown, U1800, and U1400. Players must have a valid CFC membership which they can purchase from our STORE.

Time control is 90+30. Drop-in and membership options are available.

Casual Chess

Our casual area (skittles room) opens at 6:30 and accommodates 24 players.

Kibitzing and quiet conversation are welcome.

ACC students and tournament players are welcome.

Our nightly casual drop-in fee is $10+HST payable at the front desk. Casual memberships are also available.

Classes

We have both in-person and online chess classes and courses for players of any age or skill level.

Each class involves a mix of lecture, discussion and play.

In-person classes are held at 918 Bathurst on Monday evenings starting at 5:00.

Online classes currently run on Tuesday and Thursday evenings.

Special Events

We run several special events throughout the year. Our upcoming events are the: 2023 Toronto Women's Closed Championship (new time and start date!) ACC Halloween Rapid We periodically run GM/IM norm events (e.g. our recent Canadian Transnational Chess Championship).

Benefits of Being a Member

  • Access to exclusive chess events
  • Discounts on classes and tournaments
  • Community of passionate chess players
